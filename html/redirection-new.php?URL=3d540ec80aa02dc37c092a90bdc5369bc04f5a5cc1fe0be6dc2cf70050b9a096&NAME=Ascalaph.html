Ascalaph

Molecular Modelling Suite

Freeware
GPL

  • Molecular Design
  • Molecular mechanics modelling
  • Quantum calculations
  • Force Field development


The Ascalaph molecular modelling project was named after the beautiful animal Ascalaphus, whose wonderful photographs were kindly provided by Sergey Nikolaev and Vladimir Eskin.

General Features:
  • Molecular graphics
  • Molecular model building
  • Geometry optimization
  • Molecular dynamics modelling with multistep and fourth order symplectic integrators
  • Quantum modelling using NWChem, CP2K and PC GAMESS/Firefly
  • Parallel molecular dynamics on Linux clusters with MDynaMix molecular dynamics package
